# HireNest CRM 2.0 - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Vision
HireNest CRM is the commercial nerve center for enterprise staffing and recruitment businesses. It acts as the "Business Command Center," sitting alongside the "HireNestOS Execution Engine." The CRM handles all relationships, revenue pipelines, follow-ups, and communications, while relying on the OS for the core ATS and AI workflows. It enforces a single source of truth across vendors, clients, and revenue pipelines.

## 2. Target Users
1. **Recruiters & Account Managers:** Managing accounts, vendor syncs, follow-ups.
2. **Sales Teams:** Managing the CRM revenue pipeline and driving client outreach.
3. **Vendor Managers:** Managing the health and pipeline of vendor interactions.
4. **Leadership / Executive Suite:** Gaining full visibility across revenue pipelines, recruiter productivity, and system-wide activity.

## 3. Core Modules & Specifications

### 3.1 Dashboard & Executive Visbility
- **Overview:** Executive summary of the business.
- **KPIs:** Active Accounts, Open Requirements, Active Vendors, Revenue Forecast, Conversions.
- **Live Sync:** Instant updates on activity timelines, system events, and requirements.

### 3.2. Account Management
- **Definition:** The fundamental enterprise entity (Prospects, Clients, Strategic Accounts).
- **Features:** Grouping requirements, tracking contacts, historical revenue, communication timeline.

### 3.3. Contact Management
- **Definition:** Humans representing Accounts.
- **Features:** Hierarchy definitions, direct communications (Email, WhatsApp, Calls), decision-maker tagging.

### 3.4. Requirement Pipeline
- **Definition:** Jobs requested by accounts. Tracked explicitly by commercial value.
- **Features:** Revenue Potential mapping, requirement age, status sync from OS (Submission, Interview, Offer, Join).

### 3.5. Vendor Network Management
- **Definition:** Third parties provisioning execution capability.
- **Features:** Submission metrics, response rate, placement ratio, compliance, and payments.

### 3.6. Communication Center
- **Definition:** Unified universal communication viewer (No separate channels).
- **Features:** Timelines of Email, WhatsApp, Meetings, Tasks, System Events across Clients, Vendors, and Contacts.

### 3.7. Follow-Up Center
- **Definition:** Unified tasking queue natively synced.
- **Features:** Requirement follow-ups, Candidate follow-ups, Payment follow-ups. Triggered implicitly by rules, driven explicitly by System Events.

### 3.8. Revenue Pipeline
- **Definition:** Financial progression tracker.
- **Features:** Tracks transitions natively: Requirement -> Submission -> Interview -> Offer -> Join -> Invoice -> Collection -> Revenue.

## 4. Design & UX Strategy
- **Colors:** Blue (Relationships), Green (Revenue), Orange (Follow-Ups), Purple (Comms), Red (Overdue).
- **Language:** Enterprise, standard. Strictly avoiding "Neural AI, Agent Workspace, Brain" terminology in the CRM scope. Everything should be clearly identified as standard CRM primitives. The AI and logic live in the OS.

## 5. Non-Negotiable Rules
- **Event-Driven UI:** Save Record -> Create Event -> Update Timeline -> Refresh Dashboard -> Trigger Notification -> Create Follow-Up Rule.
- **No Manual Sync:** Any changes originating in HireNestOS (such as interview creation) must update the CRM requirement timeline natively over the event bus.
